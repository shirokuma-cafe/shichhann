Hi! My name is Stephany and I am a 2D illustrator based in Queens, New York. My work has a cartoony/anime style to it, and I have proficiency in many media (wet, dry, digital). My rendering style is loosely inspired by the art of "Dragon Ball" creator Akira Toriyama and the work of manga artist/illustrator Posuka Demizu.
